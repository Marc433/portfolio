Tiris – 3D Action Platformer

Developed in Unreal Engine 5.4 as part of the final project at ESAT. I worked as one of the core gameplay programmers for this hack-and-slash action title.

Pickup & Drop System: I developed the component-based system that handles entity drops and pickups, as well as the player inventory and all power-up logic.

Enemy AI: I programmed 3 out of 6 enemy behaviors and created the base Enemy script, with configurable components and attack logic.

Raftel Engine – Custom 3D Engine

A custom graphics engine built from scratch in C++ with OpenGL. Includes a real-time editor via ImGui, lighting with shadows, entity picking, HDR & Bloom, and basic physics.

Entity System: Designed the ECS-style architecture with entity picking and transform updates.

Lighting & Visual Effects: Implemented dynamic shadows, HDR rendering, bloom, PBR lighting, and real-time post-processing.
